# Print Files

## Role
Print files are the concrete production artifacts attached to processed orders and reprints. They are stored as structured data on processed rows and are the most practical bridge between business order identity and what the printer workflow actually consumed. They matter for troubleshooting, reprint matching, PDF opening, and correlating processed orders with printer-side evidence.
